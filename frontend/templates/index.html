The Problem

Credit card fraud causes billions in losses every year. Detecting it is a classification task—every transaction must be labeled as Fraud or Legitimate. With an imbalanced dataset where only 0.17% of transactions are fraudulent, traditional machine learning methods often fail.

The Quantum Solution

Our Quantum SVM uses a 5-qubit quantum circuit to compute a quantum kernel that captures non-linear patterns in high-dimensional data. By leveraging quantum superposition and entanglement, we can detect fraud patterns that classical methods might miss.
